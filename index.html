About Me

I'm Praneeth Ravuri, a Master's student in Computer Science at George Mason University. With a background in computer science and engineering from Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology, I have a solid foundation in the field. During my academic journey, I gained practical experience through internships. As a front-end intern at Stige, I focused on designing user interfaces and creating seamless user experiences. Additionally, my role as a Software Development Engineer intern at Cognizant provided valuable insights into large-scale software development projects and strengthened my problem-solving abilities. I also contributed as a Web Developer at IEEE GRIET Student Body, where I collaborated on developing engaging web applications and platforms. I am passionate about continuous learning and staying updated with the latest advancements in technology. With a strong drive to make a meaningful impact in computer science, I aim to leverage my knowledge, skills, and experiences. My enthusiasm and dedication fuel my desire to contribute to the ever-evolving world of technology.
